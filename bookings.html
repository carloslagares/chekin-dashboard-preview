Statuses are indicated by distinctive colors to show whether a process is completed or not:
Pending: Some action is required, either from the guest or from the host.
In progress: An intermediate status between pending and completed.
Completed: No further actions are needed.
Exempted: Requirements don't apply to this booking.
Error: Some action is required from the host. Enter the booking to check what's wrong.
